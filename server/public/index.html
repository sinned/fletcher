How your privacy actually works

Not a promise — a mechanism. Here is the whole flow:

1. You are anonymous

Fletcher never asks who you are. The app registers with a random device ID — no name, email, phone number, or account. There is nothing to link your location history to you.

2. Assistants get keys, not doors

An AI assistant can only see your locations if you create a token for it — and you decide the precision it sees: exact, ~100 m, or ~1 km. Revoke a token and access stops mid-session.

3. Every access leaves a trace

Each assistant request is written to a log you can read in the app: what was asked, when, and how many locations were returned. No silent access, ever.

4. Data expires on your schedule

Pick a retention window from 7 days to forever. Old points are purged automatically — on your device and on the server. Or wipe everything, everywhere, with one tap.

No ads. No analytics. No data sales. Prefer zero trust? Run the Fletcher server yourself and your data never touches ours.
