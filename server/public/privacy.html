Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Fletcher ("we," "our," or "us") is a privacy-first location tracking app and sync server. This policy describes exactly what data Fletcher handles and how. It is written to match what the software actually does.

2. The Data We Collect

Fletcher collects the minimum needed to provide the service:

  • Location points: latitude, longitude, GPS accuracy, and timestamp. Nothing else — no altitude, speed, heading, or motion data.
  • An anonymous device identifier: a random ID generated on your device. We never ask for or store your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or any advertising identifier. There is no user account.
  • Assistant access records: when an AI assistant you connected requests your data, we log which assistant type, what was requested, when, and how many locations were returned. This log exists so you can audit access from inside the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Fletcher service to you:

  • To store and visualize your location history.
  • To answer queries from AI assistants that you have explicitly connected with a token.

We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not profile you.

4. AI Assistant Access

No assistant can see your data unless you generate a connection token in the app. You control the precision assistants receive (exact, ~100 m, or ~1 km) and how far back they can look. You can revoke a token at any time; revocation takes effect immediately, including for sessions already connected. Every request is recorded in the access log described above.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, share, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Your location data leaves the server only in response to your app or an assistant token you created.

6. Where Your Data Lives

By default the app syncs to the hosted Fletcher server. As its operators we have technical access to the database for operations and debugging, and we access it only for those purposes. If you prefer that your data never reach infrastructure we operate, you can run the Fletcher server yourself and point the app at it in Settings — self-hosted data never touches our systems.

7. Retention and Deletion

You choose the retention period, from 7 days to indefinite. Expired points are purged automatically on your device and on the server. You can also delete individual points, or delete your entire history from both the device and the server, at any time from within the app. Deletion is immediate.

8. Security

All traffic between the app, the server, and connected assistants uses HTTPS. Device credentials are stored hashed on the server and kept in the iOS Keychain on your device.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
